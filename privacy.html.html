TwistedWishes Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 23/07/2025

At TwistedWishes Ltd ("we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, https://www.thetwistedwishes.com/ (the "Site").

1. What We Collect

We do not collect personal information directly through our Site. However, we use third-party tools that may collect anonymised data:

  • Google Analytics 4 (GA4): Tracks general site traffic and user behaviour anonymously. No personally identifiable information (PII) is stored.
  • Mailing List Sign-Up Forms (via Third-Party Provider): If you choose to sign up, your email address is collected and stored securely by our email service provider, not directly by us.

2. What We Don’t Do

  • We do not use cookies or trackers for targeted advertising.
  • We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.
  • We do not store any sensitive personal information on our Site.

3. External Services

  • Our mailing list and pre-order forms are hosted through a trusted third-party service with its own privacy policy and security compliance.
  • Our analytics data is used strictly for understanding how visitors interact with our site to improve user experience.

4. Your Choices

You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using browser tools or extensions. If you sign up for our mailing list, you can unsubscribe at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or contacting us directly.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We recommend reviewing it periodically. Continued use of the Site after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance.
