Introducing: "Get Knotted"

A soft, wearable device inspired by monster romance tropes — not a gimmick, but a respectful, body-safe, emotionally intelligent answer to a fantasy that’s rarely acknowledged, let alone thoughtfully engineered.

What Makes It Different?

"Get Knotted" can be worn by the penetrating partner or on your favourite toy. It simulates the knotting phenomenon made popular through monster and shifter romance novels. Unlike other products, it’s designed to enhance sensation for both wearer and partner — though it’s a joy for solo play too.

About Us

TwistedWishes is a female-led company creating fantasy toys based on dark romance and monster themes. Led by a founder who knows this space inside-out, we’re here to bring fantasies to life with thought, tech, and a damn good story.
